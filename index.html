Biography:

ABYLA (which means Eternal, that never ends.) is Adrián Marco (main composer and guitarist), Patricia Soriano (lyricist, singer and keyboardist), Marta Soriano (drummer) and Pedro Santa (bassist). It was born in 2022 in Spain, with the idea of creating alternative, experimental rock music. With the inspiration of bands like Muse, Royal Blood or Radiohead, ABYLA published their first EP, Reborn 12/4/2024 , which contains their first singles and two unpublished songs, Escape and Reborn, their most special song which names the EP.
